General Information

What is ResQGo?

ResQGo is an on-demand ambulance service platform that connects patients with ambulance drivers. Our platform allows users to request ambulance services quickly and efficiently, with transparent pricing and real-time tracking.

How does ResQGo work?

ResQGo works in a few simple steps:

  1. Request an ambulance by entering your pickup location and destination
  2. Choose the type of ambulance service you need (Basic or Advanced Life Support)
  3. Receive fare offers from available drivers
  4. Select a driver based on their rating, ETA, and fare
  5. Track your ambulance in real-time
  6. Pay through the app or in cash after the trip

What areas does ResQGo serve?

ResQGo currently operates in Dhaka, Chittagong, and Sylhet. We are continuously expanding our service to other cities in Bangladesh. Check our app for the most up-to-date coverage information.

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

To create an account, download the ResQGo app or visit our website, click on "Register," and follow the instructions. You'll need to provide your name, phone number, email address, and create a password.

How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information by going to the "Profile" section in the app or website. Here, you can edit your personal details, add emergency contacts, and update your medical information.

Why should I add medical information to my profile?

Adding medical information to your profile helps ambulance drivers and medical professionals provide better care in case of an emergency. This information includes your blood type, allergies, medical conditions, and current medications.

Booking an Ambulance

How do I request an ambulance?

To request an ambulance, open the ResQGo app or website and follow these steps:

  1. Enter your pickup location and destination
  2. Select the type of ambulance service you need
  3. Add any special requirements or notes
  4. Click "Request Ambulance"

What types of ambulance services are available?

ResQGo offers two main types of ambulance services:

  • Basic Life Support (BLS): For non-emergency transport with basic medical equipment and trained personnel.
  • Advanced Life Support (ALS): For emergency situations requiring advanced medical equipment and paramedics.

Can I schedule an ambulance in advance?

Yes, you can schedule an ambulance in advance for non-emergency transport. On the booking screen, select "Schedule" instead of "Request Now" and choose your preferred date and time.
